About the Destination

Taman Bambu Air is a serene eco-tourism destination nestled among lush bamboo groves and tranquil waters. Our park offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and sustainable tourism, providing visitors with an authentic connection to nature.

Established with a vision to preserve the natural bamboo ecosystem while offering memorable outdoor experiences, our park has become a haven for those seeking respite from urban life.

"A calm escape by the water's edge"

We invite you to experience the gentle rustle of bamboo leaves, the soothing sounds of flowing water, and the peaceful ambiance that makes Taman Bambu Air a truly special destination.
